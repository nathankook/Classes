Valorant has rapidly grown into one of the most popular esports titles since its launch in 2020. With its unique combination of tactical shooting and character-based abilities, Valorant has attracted millions of players and viewers worldwide. Riot Games, the developer behind Valorant, has successfully organized global esports circuits, bringing together the best teams from around the world to compete for massive prize pools.

Tournaments

  • Valorant Champions Tour (VCT)

    • The VCT is Valorant’s premier global esports circuit. The competition runs throughout the year, divided into three stages: Challengers, Masters, and Champions.

    • Challengers: Regional tournaments where teams compete to qualify for the next stage.
    • Masters: International tournaments featuring the top teams from each region, battling for global dominance and points toward qualification for Valorant Champions.
    • Champions: The annual world championship, where the best teams across all regions compete for the title of World Champion.
  • Valorant Game Changers

    • Game Changers is a Riot Games initiative to create opportunities and visibility for marginalized genders in the Valorant esports scene. It provides a separate circuit for women and other underrepresented groups to compete at a high level, with regional and global events.

Esports Teams

There are many esports teams in the Valorant competitive scene all around the world, even being split up into multiple tiers. This is similar to how high school and college sports can be divided into different divisions(i.e. Division 1, Division 2). Some of the top Tier 1 esports teams include:

  • Sentinels:Sentinels, a North American team, gained fame by winning the VCT Masters Reykjavík in 2021, becoming one of the most iconic teams in Valorant esports. They are known for their aggressive playstyle and the star power of their players.
  • Fnatic:A European powerhouse in the Valorant scene, Fnatic has consistently been one of the top-performing teams. They won several major VCT events and are known for their disciplined playstyle and strategic depth.
  • LOUD:LOUD is a Brazilian team that made waves in the international scene by winning Valorant Champions 2022, solidifying their place as one of the top teams in the world.
